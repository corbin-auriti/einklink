E-ink is the technology that power e-readers like the Amazon Kindle and Kobo devices. It's what enables them to be have extremely low power consumption while helping prevent eyestrain.

There is evidence that using smartphones in the bedroom leads to insomnia. But sometimes, when in bed, we also want to know about the world around us: what's the weather going to be tomorrow? what's going on in the news?

This is where the basic built-in web browser that come with most e-readers can be so useful. However, as the average website becomes more complex and bloated sometimes they don't look so great on such a basic web browser.

Enter eink.link, a really simple website to share links to useful resources that load quickly and look good on very basic web browsers such as those that come with e-readers! This site is maintained by Doug Belshaw as a side project. You are encouraged to submit websites via the link in the footer.
